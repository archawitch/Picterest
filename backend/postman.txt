### Postman ###

1. Click 'new'
2. Paste your endpoint as 'localhost/Picterest/backend/api/[tested file]'
3. Select Body > pick raw > change data type to json > insert your tested data
4. Click 'send'

--- Example

endpoint: 'localhost/Picterest/backend/api/users.php'
method: DELETE
tested data: 
{
    "username": "johndoe"
}
output:
{
    "success": true,
    "message": "Deleted successfully"
}
